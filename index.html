BUILDING CONSTRUCTION

Building construction is the process of creating structures such as houses, schools, offices, and bridges. It begins with planning and designing by architects and engineers, followed by preparing the land and building a strong foundation. Workers then construct the walls, floors, and roof using materials like concrete, steel, bricks, and wood. After the structure is completed, electrical wiring, plumbing, painting, doors, and windows are installed. Building construction is important because it provides safe places for people to live, learn, and work, while also helping the development of communities and countries.

our facilities

At GS Rubona, there are different facilities that help students learn and enjoy school life. The school has a library where both younger and older students can go to read books and study quietly. The library helps students improve their knowledge and supports them in their studies. The school also has a playground where students play different sports and games during their free time. In addition, there is a dining area where students eat their school meals comfortably. These facilities help create a good learning environment and support students’ academic and social development.
